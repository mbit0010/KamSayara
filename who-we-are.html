Where the Idea Started

Two similar cars. Completely different prices.

The idea started from something everyone in the UAE has seen. Two similar cars, same year, same mileage, but completely different prices. It creates confusion — and nobody wants to leave a deal feeling mish murtah.

KamSayara was created to bring clarity and thiqah into this process — not by guessing, but by studying what the market is actually doing at the time you ask.

Our Position

What we are not — and why that matters

We are not a marketplace. We do not promote listings. We do not take sides. Our role is simple — to present the market in a structured and practical way so anyone can understand the real position of a car without confusion.
