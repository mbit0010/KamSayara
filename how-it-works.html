The Data

Where the numbers actually come from

KamSayara does not rely on a static database. Each valuation triggers a live scan of active UAE listings at that exact moment — so the numbers always reflect what the market is doing right now, not last month.

The system then applies filters and context to remove noise: duplicate listings, prices that fall well outside the realistic range, and inactive or stale ads that would distort the picture.
